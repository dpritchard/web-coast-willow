Coastal Otago Willows and Weavers

Formerly the Friends of the Taieri Willow

About Contact News Workshops Events Willows Resources Gallery

Coastal Otago Willows and Weavers (formerly known as the Friends of the Taieri Willow) is a group of people enthusiastic about growing and using willows for basketry. Our group was formed to:

  • Maintain the present and valuable collection of basket willow genotypes in the South Island of New Zealand;
  • Provide cuttings to NZ basketmakers of these willows to ensure the continuing survival of basket making as a craft;
  • Promote the craft of willow basket making by providing basket willow material for workshops and running workshops for beginners; and
  • Provide a working example of basket willow cultivation, harvesting and post harvest techniques.
